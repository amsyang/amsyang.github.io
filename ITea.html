ITea Cafe Pittsburgh

  • 412-688-8330
  • 5882 Forbes Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15217

This restaurant is Pittsburgh's first Chinese hot pot place, and it's also a bubble tea & Taiwanese cuisine. You can check out their menu here:Menu. You can place an order online here:Order

"They definitely win in terms of quantity--the amount of meat served is pretty insane. Second they are delicious (probably also a bit greasy but so good!), and come with a generous serving of pickled vegetables. If you are hungry, one of these meals will definitely fill you up." -- Tony Dear, Google Review

I lived on South Aiken for a year, and ITea is located right next to my house. Whenever I feel like eating out but don't have any specific food in mind, I go to ITea (If you happen to be shopping on Walnut, you can stop by as it's only about one minute walk to where the shops are located). ITea not only have hot pot, but they also have many other dishes to choose from. If you don't feel like eating out and want to order a delivery, ITea also offers hot pot delivery. Everything is well cooked and will be delivered to you while it's still hot.
